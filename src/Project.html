Project Scope

The restaurant website develops an online food ordering, delivery, and restaurant reservation service platform. Our goal is to create a single website that provides customers multiple ways to dining.

The website will include the searchable attributes to allow a customer to filter for restaurant types and food types, add dishes to the cart, and checkout and track their delivery and pickup order process. The customers can also reserve tables for a restaurant.

The restaurant owner/manager can edit the profile information, receive and confirm completion of delivery/pickup orders, receive and confirm reservation requests, and edit the menu.

The delivery guys can edit their profile information, receive and choose to accept delivery requests, and confirm completion of a delivery request.
